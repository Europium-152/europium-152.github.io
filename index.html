Day Four - Tuesday 17/07

Day Four we Drive back to Lisboa, but on the way back, we'll visit some of the best kept secrets of the Portuguese southwest coast :)

  • Monchique (35m)
  • Visit any combination of these three amazing beaches
    • Praia de Odeceixe (1h either from Alvor or Monchique). We can surf here!
    • Praia da Amália (15m)
    • Praia do Tonel (35m)
  • Cabo do Sardão (17m).
  • Lisboa (2:20h). On the way to Lisboa we'll go through some nice cities, so we can stop if we feel like it.
    • Vila Nova de Milfontes(26m)
    • Porto Côvo(21m)
    • Sines(19m)
    • And finally Lisboa (1:40h)

    The cities are worth visiting only if we have the time, because they have the same style as Lagos and Alvor so it's really nothing new. Although they are very beautiful!

    This day will have a total drive time of 5 hours. This will give us just enough time to enjoy the sites if we manage to leave Alvor early in the morning. This is really a flexible day, we'll adapt as we go along but one possiblity would be to surf in Odeceixe and then go watch the Sunset in Sardão. We'd be back in Lisbon before midnight.
